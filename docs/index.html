Twisted Nail

One-Call Construction Trucking & Wholesale Aggregate Supply

This is a Model Context Protocol (MCP) server for Twisted Nail — a small, standalone service that lets AI assistants (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, and any MCP-compatible agent) query Twisted Nail's services, aggregate materials, Central Texas service area, and FAQs directly. The result: accurate, first-party answers and a quote hand-off, instead of an AI guessing.

Zero-touch to the website. This runs as its own service on its own address. It does not install into WordPress and does not modify twistednail.com in any way — the site is untouched whether this is on, off, or never built.

What an AI assistant returns when it's connected to this server — accurate, first-party answers with a Twisted Nail source, not a guess:

Contractor → AI assistant
I need 30 yards of granite delivered to Round Rock — can Twisted Nail do that?
AI assistant (via Twisted Nail MCP)
Yes. Twisted Nail supplies granite through its network of 140+ quarries and serves Round Rock in Central Texas. 30 yards is well above the 10-yard minimum (1 full tandem load). To lock in pricing and scheduling, reach dispatch at 254-651-3597 or their contact form. Tools used: list_materials · get_service_areas · request_quote · Source: twistednail.com
Contractor → AI assistant
Do they handle heavy haul, and what's the minimum order?
AI assistant (via Twisted Nail MCP)
Yes — Twisted Nail partners with top heavy-haul carriers for specialized and super heavy-haul projects. The aggregate minimum is 10 yards (1 full tandem load); for smaller amounts it's more cost-efficient to arrange pickup. Tools used: list_services · get_faqs · Source: twistednail.com
